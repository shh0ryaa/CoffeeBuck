Refreshing Cold Coffee Drinks for Warm Days

Our Menu & Recipes

When the temperature rises in Punjab, hot espresso isn't always what you're craving. Fortunately, the world of cold coffee has evolved far beyond simply pouring hot coffee over ice. Here are our top recommendations for staying cool and energized this season.

1. The Classic Cold Brew

Unlike iced coffee, cold brew is never exposed to heat. Our baristas steep coarsely ground premium coffee beans in cool water for 12 to 24 hours. The result? A remarkably smooth, naturally sweet, and highly caffeinated beverage with almost zero acidity. It’s perfect on its own or with a splash of milk.

2. The Creamy Iced Latte

A true crowd-pleaser. The iced latte combines a bold double shot of our signature espresso with cold milk and a generous scoop of ice. It provides the rich, complex flavors of espresso but delivered in a refreshing, creamy format. For a touch of sweetness, try it with a pump of vanilla or hazelnut syrup.

3. The Indulgent Iced Mocha

For those who love chocolate, the iced mocha is the ultimate treat. We blend premium chocolate syrup with fresh espresso, cold milk, and ice. It strikes the perfect balance between a dessert and a much-needed afternoon pick-me-up. It's essentially a decadent chocolate milkshake for adults.

Ready to beat the heat? Drop by CoffeeBuck today and discover your new favorite cold coffee drink!
